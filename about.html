ABOUT US

Considering 6 major 2-Wheeler makers; the domestic sales in 2022 is approx. 13,07,132 units. The overall year growth in revenue is 8.87%. it is evident that these two Wheelers required accessories and spare parts. Since there are a lot of manufacturers available; it is difficult to find them offline or online the process is not just time consuming but can also be costly. Hence, there is a use a need to bridge this gap and provide a seamless service access to the spare parts and accessories at great prices without a lot of hustles.


Cost of bikes may vary from brand to brand, features offered utility etc. And so would the spare parts or accessories. Hence it is important to bridge the gap between bike lovers and service provider while not burning a hole in pocket. There is a need for a platform which can help local vendors expand their reach to its potential customers and vice versa. This will avoid any third-party involvement this reducing the overall cost.
